## Technical Implementation Notes

1. **Cross-Site Integration:**
   - Consistent footer section with links to sister company (WestWeld)
   - Similar design language while maintaining distinct branding
   - Cross-referrals for appropriate services
   - Reciprocal "about our companies" pages

2. **SEO Structure:**
   - Unique title tags and meta descriptions for all pages
   - Logical H1-H6 hierarchy
   - Image optimization with descriptive filenames and ALT text
   - Schema.org structured data implementation
   - XML sitemap

3. **Content Strategy:**
   - Minimum 800-1000 words of unique content per service page
   - Include relevant keyword variations naturally throughout content
   - Showcase appropriate credentials and equipment specifications
   - Include customer testimonials with schema markup
   - Before/after examples with optimized images

4. **Service Limitations Clarity:**
   - Consistent messaging across the site about not providing tree removal or logging services
   - Clear statements about what you do (brush clearing, land clearing) vs. what you don't do (tree work, logging)
   - Partner referrals for tree services when appropriate

5. **Conversion Optimization:**
   - Clear CTAs on every page
   - Click-to-call functionality for mobile users
   - Simplified quote request forms on service pages
   - Trust signals (certifications, reviews, guarantees)
   - Cross-promotion with WestWeld where appropriate
